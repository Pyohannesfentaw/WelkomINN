06 — Brand Voice
Tone & Language
WELKOMME-INN sounds like a real person running a real business. Direct, practical, local. Never corporate, never salesy.

✓ Do

Sound local, direct, and practical.

"You deal directly with me."
"No call centres, no layers, no excuses."
"We handle SEF registration and tourist tax."
"Call Pedro. Get started fast."

✗ Don't

Sound corporate, vague, or inflated.

"Leveraging synergies in property solutions"
"Your luxury escape awaits"
"We are committed to excellence"
"Our dedicated team of experts"

✓ Key Phrases

Recurring language anchors to use across all channels.

Small, dedicated team
Direct communication with Pedro
Based in Portimão
Full accountability
Faster than agencies

✗ Banned Words

Never use these in any WELKOMME-INN content.

Luxury / paradise / escape / hideaway
World-class / best-in-class / premium
Seamless / holistic / bespoke
Solutions / leverage / synergy
